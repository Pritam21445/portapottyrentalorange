Frequently Asked Questions

What's the real cost of a porta potty rental?

The cost for a porta potty is a function of the tools you choose and the service you need. The porta potty rental cost per day varies by unit type (standard vs. luxury trailer) and rental duration. We provide a quick, transparent quote based on your specific plan.

How do you help manage restroom lines at big events?

We use a combination of strategic placement, clear signage, and a smart mix of standard units and high-throughput porta urinal stations to prevent bottlenecks before they start.

Can I move the porta potty during my rental period?

Yes. For multi-phase projects, we can work with you to schedule the relocation of units to better suit the flow of your job site.

How many people can one toilet handle?

For a standard event, the rule is approximately one unit per 50 people for 4 hours. However, this changes if you're serving alcohol or the event is longer. We'll help you do the math.

What do your luxury restroom trailers require?

Our bathroom rental trailer units need access to a standard electrical outlet for lights and climate control, and a standard garden hose hookup for running water. We can provide onboard water tanks and a generator for an additional fee if needed.
